About me

Hi! My name is Taisia Khrapach, and I’m a 15-year-old professional chess player, proudly representing the Green Chess Club. I hold the first sports rank in chess and have already achieved significant milestones:

  • Bronze medalist of the Ukrainian Championship (classical chess) in the under-14 category.
  • Bronze medalist of the Ukrainian Championship (classical chess) in the under-16 category.
  • Champion of the Ivano-Frankivsk region in the under-18 category.
  • Champion of the Ivano-Frankivsk region in the under-14 category.
  • Champion of Kolomyia in the under-16 category, achieving a perfect 9 out of 9 points.
I owe my achievements to the dedicated guidance of my incredible coaches, who have not only sharpened my skills but also taught me discipline, perseverance, and the true spirit of chess. I am also deeply grateful to the founders of Green Chess, whose vision and commitment to developing young talent have created a nurturing environment where players like me can thrive.

Beyond chess, I am fluent in four languages: Ukrainian, Russian, English, and Spanish, which allows me to confidently represent my club and country internationally. Additionally, I am the author of the book "Queen’s Move", where I share my journey and insights into the chess world.

My dream is to compete on the world stage, bringing more victories to Green Chess and making my country proud. With your support, I can participate in international tournaments, access advanced training, and continue developing as an athlete.

By becoming my sponsor, you’ll not only help a young player achieve her dreams but also support the mission of Green Chess in nurturing the next generation of champions. Together, we can make a lasting impact!
